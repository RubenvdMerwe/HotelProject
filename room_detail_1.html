3-Bedroom Pool Villa

The Three-bedroom Villa with pool is the epitome of luxury island lifestyle in Mauritius. The villa features two master bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms and one room especially designed for children. Guests will be greeted by the view of their own private infinity-edge pool as they enter the villa. Each room is perfectly linked to the other. The modern open-plan kitchen extends to the lounge area which in turn opens onto the spacious terrace.

The outdoor living area, surrounded by elegant wooden decking, provides the ideal space for refined al-fresco living. This luxurious villa will offer families ample space to spend quality time together as well as retreat to their own space whenever they feel like it. Both master bedrooms look out onto the pool and garden and feature either a sumptuous outdoor bathtub or shower, shaded by a delicate gazebo and tropical greenery. A discreet corridor leads to the third room, tailored to the needs of the younger members of the family. Sophisticated browns, fresh greens and sleek wooden furniture contribute to the sense of refined comfort that reigns in this villa. Guests will truly be able to relax knowing that the dedicated services of a valet are included and a chef is available upon request. This exclusive holiday home provides the ultimate in privacy and refined island living.
